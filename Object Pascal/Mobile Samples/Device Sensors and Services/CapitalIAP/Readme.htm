Classes demonstrated

TInAppPurchase, TBannerAd
Description This demo shows the use of the TInAppPurchase and TBannerAd in order to include ads and purchasable items in your app. TBannerAd will connect to your ads services and display ads inside the app as you want. In this demo the ads will be placed at the bottom of the form and can be disabled using the TInAppPurchase component. The purchasable content must be registered in Google developer console for Android and Itunes connect for iOS and are managed by the store services. This demo demonstrates how to disable ads and how to add new content in your app, in our case European capitals questions for the trivia.
