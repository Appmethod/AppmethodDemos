
-ScanFilterServicesAdvData-

This demo shows how to scan Bluetooth low energy devices, filtering by services, 
making use of the advertiseData that most of the devices and especially the new ones are 
advertising without getting connected to them.

https://www.bluetooth.org/en-us/specification/assigned-numbers/generic-access-profile

From (0x02 to 0x07) of List of XX-bit Service Class UUIDs

This is the fastest way of scanning and the appropriate one for new BLE devices
